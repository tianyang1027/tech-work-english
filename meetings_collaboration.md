# 外企程序员常用英语 - 会议 / 协作

> 本文档整理了程序员在外企会议和团队协作中常用英语。

| 英文 | 中文意思 | 使用场景 / 例句 |
|------|----------|----------------|
| Let’s circle back | 我们稍后再讨论 | 会议中暂缓问题 |
| Could you clarify that? | 你能解释一下吗？ | 会议中不理解时 |
| Let’s take this offline | 私下讨论 | 避免会议偏题，改私下沟通 |
| Any blockers? | 有没有遇到阻碍？ | stand-up 或同步会议 |
| Let’s prioritize | 我们来排优先级 | 决策任务顺序 |
| I’ll follow up on this | 我会跟进 | 会后承诺跟进任务 |
| Keep me in the loop | 让我保持知情 | 请求被通知进展 |
| Take ownership | 负责 / 主导 | 指派任务或强调责任 |

# 外企常用会议英语（常用表达）

## 一、会议开场（Opening）
1. Let’s get started.
2. Thanks everyone for joining.
3. Shall we begin?
4. Thanks for making the time.
5. Let’s wait one more minute for others to join.
6. Is everyone able to hear me?
7. Can you see my screen?
8. Let’s start with a quick round of introductions.
9. Today’s meeting is about…
10. The goal of today’s meeting is…

## 二、会议议程（Agenda）
11. Let’s go through the agenda.
12. The first item on the agenda is…
13. Let’s move on to the next topic.
14. We’ll discuss this in more detail later.
15. Let’s stay on topic.
16. We’re a bit tight on time.
17. We’ll skip this item for now.
18. Let’s park this discussion.
19. We’ll come back to this later.
20. Does anyone want to add anything to the agenda?

## 三、发言 & 讨论（Speaking & Discussion）
21. I’d like to add something here.
22. From my perspective…
23. I agree with that.
24. I partially agree.
25. I see it differently.
26. I have a concern about this.
27. Can you clarify that point?
28. Could you elaborate on that?
29. Let me explain my reasoning.
30. Just to make sure I understand…

## 四、打断 & 插话（Interrupting Politely）
31. Sorry to interrupt.
32. Can I jump in here?
33. Just to add one thing.
34. Let me stop you there for a second.
35. May I ask a quick question?
36. Can I clarify something?
37. If I may…
38. Let me jump in real quick.
39. I’d like to challenge that assumption.
40. Let’s pause here for a moment.

## 五、技术 & 项目讨论（Technical / Project）
41. This is related to the backend logic.
42. The main challenge here is performance.
43. This may impact other services.
44. We need to consider edge cases.
45. There’s a dependency on another team.
46. We’re currently blocked by this issue.
47. This solution is scalable.
48. We need to refactor this part.
49. The root cause is still unclear.
50. We’ll need more data to decide.

## 六、同步进度（Status Updates）
51. The task is in progress.
52. We’re on track.
53. There’s a slight delay.
54. We expect to finish by EOD.
55. The issue has been resolved.
56. We’re waiting for feedback.
57. This is pending approval.
58. No major blockers at the moment.
59. We need more time on this.
60. This is our top priority right now.

## 七、提问题 & 风险（Questions & Risks）
61. Are there any risks we should be aware of?
62. What’s the impact if this is delayed?
63. Do we have a backup plan?
64. What’s blocking us right now?
65. Who owns this action item?
66. What’s the timeline for this?
67. Are there any dependencies?
68. What’s the worst-case scenario?
69. Do we need stakeholder approval?
70. Is this within the scope?

## 八、做决定（Decision Making）
71. Let’s align on this.
72. Do we agree on this approach?
73. Sounds good to me.
74. Let’s go with this solution.
75. We’ll proceed with this plan.
76. Let’s take this offline.
77. We’ll need more discussion.
78. This requires further review.
79. Let’s finalize this later.
80. Decision pending.

## 九、会议总结（Wrap-up）
81. Let’s summarize the key points.
82. To recap…
83. These are the action items.
84. Who will take this task?
85. What are the next steps?
86. Let’s confirm the owners.
87. We’ll follow up via email.
88. I’ll send out the meeting notes.
89. Let’s sync again next week.
90. Any final questions?

## 十、会议结束（Closing）
91. Thanks everyone for your time.
92. Appreciate the discussion.
93. Thanks for the input.
94. Great discussion today.
95. Thanks for joining.
96. Let’s stay in touch.
97. Talk to you next time.
98. Have a great day.
99. Enjoy the rest of your day.
100. Meeting adjourned.
