# 外企程序员常用英语 - 邮件 / 文档

> 本文档整理了程序员在外企写邮件和文档中常用英语。

| 英文 | 中文意思 | 使用场景 / 例句 |
|------|----------|----------------|
| FYI | 供参考 | 邮件里提供信息 |
| ASAP | 尽快 | 请求快速完成 |
| Please advise | 请指导 / 给意见 | 邮件请对方提供意见 |
| Let me know your thoughts | 请告诉我你的看法 | 请求反馈 |
| Thanks in advance | 先谢谢了 | 邮件结尾礼貌用语 |
| Apologies for the delay | 抱歉回复晚了 | 邮件回复晚了 |
| CC / BCC | 抄送 / 密送 | 邮件功能常用 |
| Per our discussion | 根据我们讨论 | 邮件衔接上次会议或讨论 |

# 外企程序员常用邮件英语（100句）

## 一、邮件开头（Opening）
1. Hi everyone,
2. Hi team,
3. Dear all,
4. Hello [Name],
5. Good morning.
6. Good afternoon.
7. I hope you’re doing well.
8. Hope this email finds you well.
9. I’m writing to follow up on the previous discussion.
10. I’m writing regarding the issue we discussed.

## 二、说明目的（Purpose）
11. I’m reaching out to ask about the status of the task.
12. I’d like to discuss the next steps.
13. I’m writing to request your support.
14. I’d like to share an update with you.
15. This email is to confirm our decision.
16. I’d like to clarify a few points.
17. I want to bring this issue to your attention.
18. I’m writing to get your feedback.
19. I’d like to propose a solution.
20. I’m following up on my previous email.

## 三、任务 & 进度（Task & Status）
21. The task has been completed.
22. The feature is ready for review.
23. The bug has been fixed.
24. The issue is still under investigation.
25. We are on track with the timeline.
26. The task is currently in progress.
27. This is blocked due to a dependency.
28. We expect to finish this by EOD.
29. The deployment was successful.
30. The fix will be included in the next release.

## 四、技术 & 代码相关（Technical）
31. Please find the PR attached.
32. I’ve pushed the latest changes to the branch.
33. The root cause has been identified.
34. This change may impact other modules.
35. We need to refactor this part.
36. The API response has changed.
37. I’ve added unit tests for this case.
38. The logs indicate a configuration issue.
39. We need to roll back this change.
40. The performance has improved after the fix.

## 五、请求 & 协作（Requests）
41. Could you please review this PR?
42. Could you help take a look at this issue?
43. Please let me know your thoughts.
44. I’d appreciate your feedback.
45. Could you confirm if this works for you?
46. Can you provide more details?
47. Please advise on how to proceed.
48. Could you share your availability?
49. Let me know if you have any concerns.
50. Please feel free to reach out if you have questions.

## 六、会议 & 安排（Meetings）
51. Let’s schedule a meeting to discuss this.
52. Are you available for a quick call?
53. Please find the meeting invite attached.
54. The meeting has been rescheduled.
55. I’ll send out the meeting notes shortly.
56. Let’s align on this during the next sync.
57. I’ll prepare a demo for the meeting.
58. Thanks for attending the meeting.
59. Please let me know if the time works for you.
60. We’ll follow up in the next meeting.

## 七、问题 & 风险（Issues & Risks）
61. We’ve identified a potential risk.
62. This may cause a delay.
63. We’re currently blocked by this issue.
64. The issue is intermittent.
65. This requires further investigation.
66. We need approval before proceeding.
67. There’s a dependency on another team.
68. This is outside the current scope.
69. We may need to revisit the design.
70. I’ll keep you posted on the progress.

## 八、确认 & 回复（Confirmation）
71. Thanks for the update.
72. Noted, thank you.
73. Sounds good to me.
74. That works for me.
75. I agree with this approach.
76. Confirmed.
77. Thanks for confirming.
78. Acknowledged.
79. I’ll take care of this.
80. Understood.

## 九、结尾（Closing）
81. Thanks for your support.
82. Thank you for your time.
83. Appreciate your help.
84. Looking forward to your reply.
85. Please let me know if you need anything else.
86. Happy to discuss further.
87. Let me know your thoughts.
88. Thanks in advance.
89. Have a great day.
90. Best regards,
